Instructions and Interactivity options:

Automatically add new birds:

To automatically add new birds into the simulation, simply hold down the mouse mover the canvas. Make sure to move the mouse around!

Changing values:

Slider 1 - Change alignment

Slider 2 - Change cohesion

Slider 3 - Change separation

Add/Remove birds

Add - To add new birds simply press the "Add Birds" button

Remove - To remove birds simplt press the "Remove Birds" button

Rainbow mode

To change all the birds multicoloured press the "Rainbow Mode" button. Once finished press the button again to deactivate.

Mute Sound

To mute the simulation simply press the mute button. Sounds will be reactivated again when you click on the canvas.

Reset Simulation

To reset the simulation back to its default format, press the reset button
